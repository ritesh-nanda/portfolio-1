Projects

VSLV

India’s first undergraduate and technical student body to design a satellite launch vehicle that holds the LIMCA and INDIA book of records. The main purpose of the project is to launch a pico-sat to monitor the slit concentration and cracks in India’s Longest Hirakud Dam. The project is felicitated by the Government of India and also been supervised by ISRO.

Soldier Strap

Communication is a vital parameter for soldiers. But the present communication system used among soldiers i.e. walkie-talkie can easily be hackable as it uses frequency for operation. Our soldier strap creates a mesh network and encrypts the signal that makes it unbreachable. It also features a concrete connection with the instruction headquarters to ensure quick real-time information from each army unit.

Variable Intensity Light

It’s a LED bulb that uses the colour temperature property of light to mix the warm and cool white light that has the advantages of both Sodium light and normal LED bulb. The IoT ensures the control of the light from anywhere through the design mobile app. The AI ensures the automatic adjustment of intensity based on the surrounding light and presence of the number of people or according to the job done by the person(s).

Text Sentiment Analysis

A computer program that can analyse the user sentiments through the text input. Developed using MATLAB, exported as an engine for its implementation in python for real world application.

Solar Miura Fold Panels

Different models solar panel designed, configured with robotic movement such that the panels can adjust their configurations to get the maximum intensity of sunlight using the different configurations. Our model has achieved, 30% more solar power generation than existing model and the design also reduced the area occupied by the panel by 10%.

Cold Storage

An affordable, scalable, easily transportable and non-electric storage unit which was developed for the farmers with a purpose of storing agricultural products in hot and humid climate conditions to preserve the freshness of their crops. The product uses the natural mechanism of evaporation like in earthen pots, keeping the items inside cool and fresh for a longer period of time. The lab test conducted under certain atmospheric conditions have achieved the lowest temperature of 14°C.
